HTT is your competent partner

For Thermal Oil Heaters, Thermal Oil Units, Heat Recovery, And Plant Modernisation

Specifically adapted thermal oil heaters, thermal oil boilers and thermal oil units built by HTT solve every process heat problem. They are flexible as to their use, individually designed, environmentally friendly, and economic.

HTT delivers energy-efficient solutions for heating and cooling purposes by fired and electrically heated thermal oil heaters, heat exchangers or heat recovery systems. Whether as large plant or turn-key unit for direct use, as new plant or plant to be modernized: the modern process tempering by means of thermal oil heaters and heat recovery as it is understood by HTT and delivered, offers a wide range of possibilities.

Products

From small to large – from ice-cold to hot

HTT units are fired with liquid, gaseous and solid fuels or heated electronically – precisely to the required degree for all temperature ranges: For warm water up to +100°C, for hot water up to +240°C, for thermal oil up to +400°C. Our combined heating and cooling systems provide for cold temperatures down to -120°C. In addition, HTT steam generators and secondary heating circuits are able to deliver independent, individually set temperatures. WHS, the subsidiary company complements the offer with heat exchangers and complete heat recovery units.
